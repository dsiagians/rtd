RTD FC

Est. 2007 - Metro Jaya's Pride

Club History

RTD Football Club Metro Jaya was established in 2007 with the vision of becoming one of the leading football clubs in the Metro Jaya region. The club was founded by a group of football enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for local talent to shine. Starting from humble beginnings in local competitions, the club has grown significantly over the years, developing both its infrastructure and player development programs. The club's signature black and gold colors represent excellence and prestige, while the three stars above the logo symbolize our core values: Respect, Teamwork, and Dedication.

Vision

Today, RTD FC continues to be a symbol of footballing excellence in Metro Jaya, focusing on developing local talent and contributing to the community through various football development programs.

Major Achievements

  • Kapolda Cup (2015)
  • Kapolri Cup (2018)
  • Piala President (2022)
